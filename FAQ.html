FAQ

Can I learn more than 1 instrument?

Yes, but you will have to master the instrument that you chose before.

Is there any limit?

Yes attendents must be a student of APU

I have no experience playing in an orchestra, can I still join

Yes. The band welcomes all students regardless of experience and background.

Do I have to pay a fee for the band?

No,all instrument is supplied by APU but you are free to bring your own instrument
