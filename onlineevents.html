  • 2022 Mini Orchestra Concert

    Objective:

    1. To gain more experience
    2. Consolidate the pitch and rhythm of the various instrumental parts in the ensemble
    3. Building out our audience

    Date: 5/8/2022 (Sunday)

    Time: 7:00pm-9.30pm

    Performance Music:

    "Symphony No.2"
    "Symphony No.6"
    "Serenade No.13 in G Major"
    "Introduction, or Sunrise"
    "Messiah"

    Collaboration is at the heart of every school band. For introverted students, the school band is a great way for them to step out of their comfort zone and connect with peers who have similar interests. Providing a nurturing environment such as the school band can also help students become more confident in their social skills — an important trait that is useful throughout life.

  • Malaysia International Online Wind Band Competition (MIVBC)

    Date: 11/10/2021

    Medium: Online Recording

    Performance Music:

    "Hungarian Dances"
    "Symphony No.1"
    "Serenade No.13 in G Major"

    Participating in the competition gave the group a common goal to work towards and pushed the group to practice hard in order to perform better in the competition. The main objective of the competition was to sharpen the group's playing skills and to enable them to work together as a group and as a large ensemble, adapting to the various forms of music presented.
