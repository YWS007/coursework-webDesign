Dr Gu Fang Yi (born January 21, 1993) has composed many masterpieces for symphony orchestras which were performed by bigger orchestras such as orchestras from Spain, the National Symphony Orchestra and the Kuala Lumpur Symphony Orchestra. He works as Freelance APU Orchestra Band Coah(2005-Present).

Fan Yi Long is a graduate with first-class honours from UCSI University’s Institute of Music, he organised and led the APU University Orchestra Tour in 2018 and conducted the orchestra during a showcase as well.
